============================================================


HOOK (0:00 – 0:15)
-------------------

"Mike is a developer. He wants to build a site that validates startup ideas.

But instead of coding one big AI that does everything —
he wants his product to always use the best available specialist for each task.

Market analysis? The top-ranked market research agent.
Customer profiling? The highest-scoring persona agent.
Not hardcoded. Not fixed. The best one. Today."
